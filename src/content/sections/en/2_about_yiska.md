Jazz & Jesus... That's Yiska in 2 words. With her music, she brings a little church to the jazzclub and some jazz to the church. Her music is influenced by jazz and gospel with lyrics about real love and real faith.

Yiska studied Jazz Vocals at the Royal Conservatoire in the Hague. She currently works as a session vocalist in the jazz and Christian music scene, and teaches voice and music lessons.
